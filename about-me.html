My life till now

I would say that I lived a nomadic lifestyle for the great majority of my childhood. From constantly changing continents, countries, cities and towns. It has allowed me to experience a small part of this vast world, which has truly captivated me, since the start of this journey. I was born in New Zealand, Auckland but before I knew it my mums took me on a crazy adventure of backpacking through the world. I meet many people and learned to call home various places. Mainly I was moulded by Spain, England and New Zealand; being primarly the countries were I resided the most.

The idea of knoweldge fascinates me tremendously and I truly beleive I am going to an eternal student in this life.
